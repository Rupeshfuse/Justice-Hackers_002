Design Elements of the 2030s

The 2030s speculate on future design trends driven by emerging technologies and user expectations. Key concepts include:

  • Paper Morphism: Hypothetical design trend mimicking the texture and behavior of paper in digital interfaces.
  • High Contrast Interfaces: Bold designs with high contrast for enhanced readability and visual impact.
  • Emerging Technologies: Exploration of AR/VR integrations and AI-driven interfaces, shaping next-generation user experiences.

Paper Morphism

Paper morphism is a design trend mimicking the physical properties of materials like paper, with depth and texture effects.
